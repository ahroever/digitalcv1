My Dogs!

About my dogs

I have three dogs and their names are Luna, Cole, and Shea. Cole and Shea are in the picture to the right on top and Luna is the bottom image. Cole is a rescue from West Virginia, we named him this because it looks like he came out of a coal mine. Shea is named after the New York Mets old baseball stadium, Shea Stadium. My family are massive New York Mets fans so we have named multiple animals after Mets players. One of my cats is named Mookie after the player Mookie Wilson. Luna is named after the Harry Potter charachter Luna Lovegood. I got Luna for my 16th birthday but she now prefers my mom over me.

Other pets I've had

In my life I have had 7 dogs and 4 cats who have all lived and are living long happy lives. Mookie and Freddie are my two cats that are alive currently. Mookie is an older cat and Freddie is only a few years old so Mookie gets annoyed with Freddie sometimes. My dad wasn't allowed to have dogs growing up so he is now making up for all that lost time by getting lots of dogs. One of the other dogs I had that passed away was a toy poodle named Mimi who was my baby. I got her in fourth grade and she unfortunately died suddenly when I was in eighth grade. That was a very upsetting death for me but with time I was able to heal and get my new dog Luna!

I hope you have liked learning about my dogs!
